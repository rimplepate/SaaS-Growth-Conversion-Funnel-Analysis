# SaaS-Growth-Conversion-Funnel-Analysis
🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes a simulated SaaS platform dataset to evaluate user behavior across the acquisition and revenue funnel.

The goal was to:
Identify drop-off points in the user journey
Measure conversion performance by acquisition channel
Evaluate A/B experiment impact
Analyze revenue and ARPU trends
Perform cohort retention analysis

The project simulates real-world product analytics work performed by a Product or Growth Data Analyst.

🏢 Business Scenario

The company (InsightLoop AI – a subscription-based SaaS platform) wanted to understand:
Where users drop off in the onboarding funnel
Which acquisition channels drive high-value customers
Whether experiment variant B improves paid conversion
How retention trends differ by signup cohort

📂 Dataset Structure

The dataset consists of three relational tables:

1️⃣ users.csv
2️⃣ funnel_events.csv
3️⃣ subscriptions.csv

📈 Key Analyses Performed
🔹 Funnel Analysis

Calculated stage-by-stage user counts
Measured overall conversion rate (Signup → Paid)
Identified major drop-off at Trial → Subscription stage

🔹 Acquisition Channel Performance

Compared conversion rate by channel
Evaluated ARPU by traffic source
Identified high-value acquisition channels

🔹 A/B Test Evaluation

Compared conversion rates between Experiment Group A and B
Measured uplift in paid conversion

🔹 Revenue Analysis

Calculated Total Revenue
Computed ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
Segmented revenue by acquisition channel

🔹 Cohort Retention Analysis

Built monthly signup cohorts

💡 Key Insights

Significant user drop-off observed at the Trial → Subscription stage
LinkedIn Ads showed higher conversion compared to Organic traffic
Experiment Variant B demonstrated improved paid conversion
Revenue contribution varied by acquisition channel
Cohort analysis revealed declining retention in later months

📌 Business Recommendations

Optimize onboarding during trial stage to reduce drop-off
Allocate more budget toward high-converting channels
Deploy winning experiment variant to all users
Improve feature adoption early in user lifecycle
